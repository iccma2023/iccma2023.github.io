ICCMA 2023

5th International Competition on Computational Models of Argumentation

The series of International Competition on Computational Models of Argumentation (ICCMA) aims at nurturing research and development of implementations for computational models of argumentation.

The 5th ICCMA competition, taking place in 2023, will be organized by:

  • Matti Järvisalo (University of Helsinki, Finland)
  • Tuomo Lehtonen (University of Helsinki, Finland)
  • Andreas Niskanen (University of Helsinki, Finland)

More information on ICCMA 2023 will be made available later on. Stay tuned!
